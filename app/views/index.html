What's coming next

Our Design System will be a place for reusable things - like components, patterns - and advice - that are 'proven to work', so they can be used to create, at greater speed, high quality work that solves problems for users and the organisation.

We've been in a phase of collecting the work done in teams - the designs in use, the thinking beneath, and how well we know if they work. Creating a more 'familiar' looking design system will occur in this next phase, as we refine what we have collected.

The high level plan is, incrementally and iteratively:

  • publish advice as soon as it's good enough
  • work on the next most important items that people need
  • repeat

Detail of the next phase is being worked, will be published here, and will also show:

  • how we decide what constitutes 'proven' - what is 'good enough'?
  • where do we publish information and assets - where people will consume it most easily
  • how do we turn this into a living thing, that many practices contribute to and use?
