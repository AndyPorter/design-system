What's new here?

Introducing the Design system

We are working towards a Design System for HM Land Registry digital teams - as most people will imagine it - a visible library of designs and guidance.

This is the start of publishing a body of reviewed, agreed and evidenced advice and patterns. The previous introduction (not widely shared) outlines where we were at in August

Knowing what's what

The site will be the only link you need to find things out: the latest advice to follow, how to contribute, whats going on with the design system and whats already happenned. Work in the open.

Helping out

The system will be most successful if it's a whole (and cross) practice thing. There is no master blueprint of what that this all looks like, which we are implementing piecemeal. This is an agile, iterative, approach toward some desired outcomes and goals, using principles like continuous feedback.

Get involved or give feedback at any time, on anything (email to Andy and Laura). We'll highlight in this system and advice docuentation where specific help is needed from people.

Hierarchy of advice

The hierarchy of advice to follow when creating new services, products or systems, for GOV.UK services, professional users and staff.
